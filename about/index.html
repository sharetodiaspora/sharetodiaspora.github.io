Advanced Sharer for diaspora*

Share all teh internetz!

Features

  • Share websites on any diaspora* pod. Popular pods already available on a list.
  • Optionally memorize your last 3 pods.
  • URL shortening (via j.mp).
  • Markdown formatting.
  • Privacy: all user data is stored on the browser local storage. Shared URL will be shared with j.mp (from Bit.ly) only when URL shortening is enabled.

This method will include a diaspora* share button next to Google +1 button. It is necessary to enable the sharing buttons.
  • Enter your dashboard and choose your blog.
  • In section Template, click on Edit HTML. If a warning appears, click Continue.
  • Check Expand widget templates, press Ctrl+F and write sharebuttons.
  • Find the line <b:includable id='shareButtons' var='post'>. Just after that, copy the following code:
<a expr:href='"http://sharetodiaspora.github.io/?url=" + data:post.url + "&title=" + data:post.title' target='_blank' title='Share on diaspora'>
	<img src='http://sharetodiaspora.github.io/favicon.png' style='width:16px; height:16px; padding:0; border:0; vertical-align:middle;'/>
</a>
Share on diaspora* Plugin, by Vitalie Ciubotaru, inserts a Share on diaspora* button onto your Wordpress blog.
This method will include a diaspora* share button under the entry's tag list.
  • In your blog's admin panel go to Templates in the section Design
  • Edit the Entry template
  • Inside the <div> tag for the asset-footer, after asset-footer-tags add this snippet: 
    <p class="asset-footer-share">
    <a href="https://sharetodiaspora.github.io/?url=<$mt:EntryPermalink encode_url="1"$>&title=<$mt:EntryTitle encode_url="1"$>" target="_blank" title="Share on diaspora" style="padding: 3px 4px 2px; background: #fafafa; border: 1px solid #ddd; color: #222; text-decoration: none;">

Add to Firefox

Firefox now supports diaspora* as a sharing service! These are other alternatives to share links from Firefox:
  • Add a native Share button to the Share Services of Firefox. Click "Share This Page" from the browser toolbar to open the save dialog. Just click this button and then choose Enable Services.
  • Add as an addon: diaspora* Advanced Sharer by Jason Robinson.
